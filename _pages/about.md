As an agronomist, I am interested in issues surrounding climate change and the sustainable management of natural resources and the environment.
I have a great passion for remote sensing of natural resources and GIS, which led me to programming in R and Python during my graduation project. I have built on my 5 years of training at IAV and my experiences as an intern on these topics and on the challenge of food security in Africa.
My involvement in various internships and associations have strengthened my ability to work in a team in a multicultural environment and to enhance my self-improvement. Results-oriented and determined, I have excellent writing skills in French and English.
